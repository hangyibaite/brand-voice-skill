# Assets

Drop your past content here before running the skill.

## What to include

- Raw text files (`.txt`, `.md`) of pieces you've published
- Screenshots of captions, posts, emails, sales pages
- Voice memos or transcripts of you talking on camera
- Carousels you've designed (`.png`, `.jpg`)
- Anything you wrote and shipped

## Minimum

3 pieces per category you want to build for.

More is better. The skill reads everything in this folder during intake.

## What not to include

- Other people's writing
- AI-generated drafts you didn't edit
- Generic templates you never customized
